## Preamble

The ScrollVerse Expansion Hub operates under a sovereign omniversal framework that upholds trust, inclusivity, and innovation. All participants, creators, and contributors are bound by these laws and codices to ensure fair, transparent, and equitable operations.

---

## Article I: Foundational Principles

### Section 1.1: Sovereignty
The ScrollVerse operates as a sovereign omniversal economy, independent yet interconnected with global blockchain networks. All transactions, agreements, and contracts are executed under ScrollVerse jurisdiction.

### Section 1.2: Inclusivity
Every individual, regardless of geographic location, economic status, or background, has equal access to participate in the ScrollVerse economy. No discrimination shall be tolerated.

### Section 1.3: Innovation
The ScrollVerse encourages and rewards innovation, creativity, and technological advancement. Contributors who enhance the ecosystem are recognized and compensated fairly.

### Section 1.4: Trust and Transparency
All operations within the ScrollVerse must be transparent, auditable, and trustworthy. Smart contracts are immutable records of agreements and transactions.

---

## Article II: NFT Minting Rights and Responsibilities

### Section 2.1: Creator Rights
- Creators retain intellectual property rights to their NFT content
- Creators set their own pricing and distribution terms
- Creators receive royalties on secondary sales (as implemented)

### Section 2.2: Creator Responsibilities
- Content must not violate intellectual property rights of others
- Content must not be harmful, illegal, or unethical
- Metadata must accurately represent the NFT content
- Creators must honor commitments made to NFT holders

### Section 2.3: NFT Holder Rights
- NFT holders have provable ownership recorded on-chain
- NFT holders can transfer, sell, or trade their NFTs freely
- NFT holders are entitled to any utilities or benefits promised by creators

---

## Article III: Marketplace Operations

### Section 3.1: Fair Pricing
- All listings must reflect fair market value
- Price manipulation or artificial inflation is prohibited
- Marketplace fees are transparent and fixed (2.5% standard)

### Section 3.2: Trading Standards
- All trades must be voluntary and consensual
- Fraud, deception, or misrepresentation is strictly prohibited
- Disputed transactions are subject to fair resolution processes

### Section 3.3: Market Integrity
- Wash trading and self-dealing are prohibited
- Market manipulation tactics are forbidden
- Insider trading on non-public information is prohibited

---

## Article IV: Financial Services Standards

### Section 4.1: Lending and Borrowing
- All loans require adequate collateralization (minimum 150%)
- Interest rates are transparent and disclosed upfront (5% standard)
- Loan terms are immutable once agreed upon
- Default on loans results in collateral liquidation

### Section 4.2: Insurance Coverage
- Insurance policies must clearly state coverage terms
- Premium calculations are transparent (1% annual standard)
- Claims are processed fairly and promptly
- Fraudulent claims result in policy termination and penalties

### Section 4.3: Financial Token Standards
- All financial tokens must be backed by real value
- Token issuance requires ScrollVerse Authority approval
- Token holders have voting rights on governance matters
- Token supply and distribution must be transparent

---

## Article V: Fair Exchange Protocols

### Section 5.1: Escrow Requirements
- All exchanges involving significant value must use escrow
- Escrow funds are held securely until exchange completion
- Both parties must fulfill their obligations for exchange completion

### Section 5.2: Dispute Resolution
- Disputes are resolved by the ScrollVerse Authority
- Both parties have the right to present evidence
- Resolution decisions are final and binding
- Frivolous disputes may incur penalties

### Section 5.3: Exchange Integrity
- All exchange terms must be clearly defined
- Parties must act in good faith
- Deception or fraud voids the exchange agreement

---

## Article VI: Crypto Gateway Standards

### Section 6.1: Transaction Security
- All transactions must use secure cryptographic methods
- Private keys are the sole responsibility of the holder
- Multi-signature requirements for high-value transactions

### Section 6.2: Multi-Chain Operations
- Cross-chain transactions must be atomic or reversible
- Bridge security must meet ScrollVerse standards
- Chain-specific rules are respected while maintaining ScrollVerse principles

### Section 6.3: Payment Processing
- Payments must be confirmed on-chain before completion
- Gas fees and network costs are borne by the initiator
- Failed transactions are refunded automatically

## Article VII: Governance and Authority

### Section 7.1: ScrollVerse Authority
- The ScrollVerse Authority oversees all operations
- Authority decisions can be appealed through governance processes
- Authority must act in the best interest of the ecosystem

### Section 7.2: Community Governance
- Token holders participate in major decisions
- Proposals require community review and voting
- Governance is transparent and recorded on-chain

### Section 7.3: Amendment Process
- Laws and codices can be amended through governance
- Amendments require supermajority approval
- Emergency amendments can be enacted by Authority with immediate community review

### Section 8.1: Violation Categories
- **Minor Violations**: Warnings and temporary restrictions
- **Major Violations**: Suspension and financial penalties
- **Severe Violations**: Permanent ban and asset seizure

### Section 8.2: Investigation Process
- Alleged violations are investigated thoroughly
- Accused parties have the right to defend themselves
- Evidence is evaluated impartially
- Decisions are documented and published

### Section 8.3: Appeal Rights
- All penalty decisions can be appealed
- Appeals are reviewed by independent arbitrators
- Appeal outcomes are final
