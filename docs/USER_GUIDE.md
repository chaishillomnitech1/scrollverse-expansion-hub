## NFT Minting

### How to Mint an NFT

1. **Prepare Your Content**
   - Create your digital artwork, music, or other content
   - Upload to IPFS or another decentralized storage service
   - Obtain the content URI (e.g., `ipfs://QmX...`)

2. **Mint Your NFT**
   - Navigate to the NFT Minting section
   - Click "Mint New NFT"
   - Enter the recipient address (your address or another)
   - Provide the metadata URI
   - Confirm the transaction in your wallet
   - Wait for blockchain confirmation

3. **View Your NFT**
   - Your minted NFT will appear in your collection
   - The token ID will be generated automatically
   - You can view metadata and ownership details

### NFT Metadata Best Practices

```json
{
  "name": "Your NFT Name",
  "description": "Description of your NFT",
  "image": "ipfs://QmX.../image.png",
  "attributes": [
    {
      "trait_type": "Category",
      "value": "Art"
    },
    {
      "trait_type": "Rarity",
      "value": "Rare"
    }
  ]
}
```

## Marketplace Trading

### Listing an NFT for Sale

1. Navigate to "My NFTs"
2. Select the NFT you want to sell
3. Click "List for Sale"
4. Set your price in the native cryptocurrency
5. Confirm the listing transaction
6. Your NFT will appear in the marketplace

### Buying an NFT

1. Browse the marketplace
2. Click on an NFT to view details
3. Review price, seller, and metadata
4. Click "Buy Now"
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
6. The NFT will be transferred to your wallet upon confirmation

### Making Offers

1. Navigate to an NFT listing
2. Click "Make Offer"
3. Enter your offer amount
4. Send the offer (funds will be held in escrow)
5. Wait for the seller to accept or reject
6. If accepted, the NFT transfers to you; if rejected, funds are returned

### Withdrawing Earnings

1. Navigate to "My Earnings"
2. View your accumulated sales
3. Click "Withdraw Earnings"
4. Confirm the transaction
5. Funds will be transferred to your wallet

**Note:** A 2.5% marketplace fee applies to all sales.

## Digital Banking

### Depositing Funds

1. Navigate to the Digital Bank section
2. Click "Deposit"
3. Enter the amount to deposit
4. Confirm the transaction
5. Your balance will be updated

### Requesting a Loan

1. Navigate to "Loans"
2. Click "Request Loan"
3. Enter the loan amount (principal)
4. Enter the loan duration in days
5. Deposit collateral (minimum 150% of principal)
6. Confirm the transaction
7. Loan funds will be transferred to your wallet

**Loan Terms:**
- Interest Rate: 5% of principal
- Minimum Collateral: 150% of principal
- Early repayment allowed
- Default results in collateral forfeiture

### Repaying a Loan

1. Navigate to "My Loans"
2. Select the loan to repay
3. Click "Repay Loan"
4. Send payment (principal + interest)
5. Confirm the transaction
6. Collateral will be returned upon confirmation

### Purchasing Insurance

1. Navigate to "Insurance"
2. Click "Purchase Policy"
3. Enter coverage amount
4. Enter policy duration in days
5. Review premium calculation (1% per year)
6. Confirm the transaction
7. Policy will be activated

**Insurance Features:**
- Transparent premium calculation
- Flexible coverage amounts
- Defined policy periods
- Claim processing by ScrollVerse Authority

## Fair Exchange System

### Creating an Exchange

1. Navigate to "Fair Exchange"
2. Click "Create Exchange"
3. Enter the other party's address
4. Define what you will provide (amount)
5. Define what you will receive (amount)
6. Add a description of the exchange
7. Confirm the transaction

### Funding an Exchange

1. Navigate to "My Exchanges"
2. Select the exchange to fund
3. Click "Fund Exchange"
4. Send the agreed amount
5. Funds will be held in escrow
6. Wait for the other party to fund their side

### Completing an Exchange

Once both parties have funded the exchange:
1. Review the exchange details
2. Click "Complete Exchange"
3. Confirm the transaction
4. Funds will be distributed to both parties

### Raising a Dispute

If there's an issue with an exchange:
1. Navigate to the exchange details
2. Click "Raise Dispute"
3. Provide a detailed reason
4. Submit the dispute
5. ScrollVerse Authority will review and resolve

**Note:** A 0.5% dispute resolution fee applies.

## Best Practices

### Security
- **Never share your private keys**
- Use hardware wallets for large amounts
- Enable two-factor authentication where available
- Verify contract addresses before interacting
- Review all transactions before confirming

### NFT Creation
- Use high-quality content
- Store metadata on decentralized storage (IPFS)
- Provide accurate descriptions
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Consider environmental impact

### Trading
- Research before buying
- Verify NFT authenticity
- Understand marketplace fees
- Use fair exchange for large transactions
- Report suspicious activity

### Financial Services
- Only borrow what you can repay
- Maintain adequate collateral
- Understand interest rates and fees
- Keep track of loan due dates
- Purchase insurance for valuable assets

## FAQs

**Q: What is the ScrollVerse Expansion Hub?**
A: It's a comprehensive platform for NFT minting, trading, financial services, and fair exchanges in a sovereign omniversal economy.

**Q: What fees are charged?**
A: 
- Marketplace: 2.5%
- Loans: 5% interest
- Insurance: 1% per year
- Dispute Resolution: 0.5%

**Q: How do I get support?**
A: Contact the ScrollVerse Authority through the official channels or community forums.

**Q: Can I cancel a transaction?**
A: Once confirmed on the blockchain, transactions are immutable. Use the Fair Exchange system for reversible agreements.

**Q: What happens if I default on a loan?**
A: Your collateral will be forfeited to cover the loan amount.

**Q: How are disputes resolved?**
A: The ScrollVerse Authority reviews evidence from both parties and makes a binding decision.

**Q: Are my funds safe?**
A: Smart contracts are audited for security, but always practice good security hygiene and never invest more than you can afford to lose.

**Q: Can I create multiple NFTs at once?**
A: Yes, you can mint multiple NFTs by calling the mint function multiple times.

**Q: What blockchain is this built on?**
A: The system is designed to be blockchain-agnostic and can be deployed on Ethereum, Polygon, BSC, and other EVM-compatible chains.
